About Us

At LJ, we are passionate about bringing the beauty of nature's finest treasures to you. Specializing in ethically sourced gems, our collection features a stunning array of unique pieces that celebrate individuality and elegance. With a commitment to quality and exceptional customer service, we strive to connect each gem to its new owner, ensuring a truly personal experience. Join us in exploring the captivating world of gemstones and discover the perfect addition to your collection! Our Story The journey of LJ Gem Store began with a simple yet profound moment in the heart of a vibrant, bustling market in Jaipur, India. Lee Joshua, affectionately known as LJ, was a young traveler with a deep appreciation for the beauty of nature and an adventurous spirit. While exploring the rich hues of the local gems and learning about their origins, he had a life-changing revelation: gems are not just beautiful objects; they carry stories of the earth and the hands that have touched them. Inspired by this realization, LJ decided to embark on a quest to bring ethically sourced gemstones to the world. He traveled across continents, from the serene beaches of Bali to the rugged terrains of South America, meeting artisans and miners who shared their passion for honest and ethical gem sourcing. These experiences fueled his commitment to sustainability and transparency in the gem industry. With a vision to create a space where people could not only find exquisite gems but also connect with their stories, LJ founded LJ Gem Store.
